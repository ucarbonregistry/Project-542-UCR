The project activity is titled- “50 MW Solar Project by Sunshakti Solar Power Projects Pvt Ltd in
Telangana”. It is a solar-power Project which is located in Pedayyipally, Kamareddy District in
the South side of Hyderabad in Telengana, India. The project activity aims to harness solar
radiation, a renewable energy source, to generate electricity for supply to Telangana grid by the
project proponent (PP), M/S Sunshakti Solar Power projects private Limited. The project
consists of ground mounted photo voltaic solar plant with aggregated installed capacity of 50
MW, located in Telangana, India, to generate clean energy and reduce greenhouse gas (GHG)
emissions. This project comprises of solar panels spread across Pedayyipally village.
The solar project generates approximately 631,307 MWh of clean electricity in the current
monitoring period. The power produced by the 50MW is evacuated at 132 KV level with
Interconnection Point at 220/132 KV Kamareddy SS.

The generated Power is being sold to Telangana State Northern Power Distribution Company
Limited (TSNPDCL). This process ensures the generated solar power is integrated into the
larger power supply system. The power purchase agreement is valid for a period of 20 years,
aligned with the operational lifetime of the project activity, and outlines the terms and conditions
for energy injection, transmission losses, scheduling, and settlement.

The expected operational lifetime of the project is for 25 years. Addressing the energy demandsupply gap in Telangana and supporting the region’s sustainable growth.
The solar plant has been operational since 02/11/2017. The project has been operational since
the earliest commissioning date.

This project activity was not registered in any other registries prior to its registration in UCR. PP
seeks verification under UCR from 02/11/2017 onwards, i.e., crediting period for UCR starts
from 02/11/2017. Hence, there is no double counting for said projects.
___________
The Crediting Period of the Project activity is 7 years from 02/11/2017 to 31/12/2024 in which total
Actual electricity generation is 631,307 MWh and total GHG emission reduction is 554,625tCO2e.
with the average plant load factor of 21.64%. This annual average net electricity generation and
annual average GHG emission reductions are with application of degradation factor of 0.5% from
second year.
